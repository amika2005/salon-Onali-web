About Onali

Since 2015, Salon Onali has been Kurunegala's premier destination for beauty and self-care. Our philosophy is simple: every client deserves personalized attention and transformative results.

Founded by master stylist Onali Perera, our salon combines traditional techniques with contemporary trends to create looks that enhance your natural beauty while expressing your unique personality.
